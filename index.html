Angular Defect Orientation Detection from B-scans

GPR scans for sharp angular defects in low-permittivity-contrast building envelopes are simulated using an FDTD solver (gprMax). A convolutional regressor is subsequently applied to predict the angle of orientation for the defects by analyzing the GPR data.

Dataset

Defects with different orientations are simulated.

Reduced embedding reveals discernible trends indicating raw data may be sufficient for prediction.
